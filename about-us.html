Amy Ann Living was created with a simple intention: to design outdoor spaces that feel like escape.

We believe furniture should not simply occupy a terrace — it should define its atmosphere. Framing views, catching light, inviting pause.

Each collection of luxury outdoor furniture is shaped by proportion, balance and the quiet confidence of considered design — bringing architectural presence to villas, terraces and refined garden spaces.

We Design Atmosphere

Furniture is never just functional. It sets the tone of a space.

Our pieces are informed by landscape, architecture and the rhythm of coastal living — creating environments that feel structured yet inviting.

From sculpted teak frames to powder-coated aluminium structures and tactile woven fabrics, every detail is resolved with intention — elevating premium outdoor seating into something quietly exceptional.

Built for Permanence

We work with materials that evolve beautifully over time.

Our collections are crafted using solid teak, architectural aluminium and premium outdoor fabrics — selected not only for their aesthetic depth, but for their ability to endure climate, sun and season.

Designed as high-end outdoor furniture for terraces, poolside settings and contemporary gardens, each piece is engineered for structural integrity while maintaining the softness and comfort expected of interior design.

Amy Ann Living is not about trend or spectacle — it is about longevity, presence and outdoor spaces designed to live with you for years to come.

Sustainability

We believe true luxury is enduring. Our materials are responsibly sourced and selected for their natural longevity, ensuring each piece ages with quiet grace. Designed to transcend seasons, our collections are created with permanence in mind.

Craftsmanship

Every piece reflects a dedication to proportion, balance and hand-finished detail. Sculpted frames, carefully tensioned weaves and considered textures come together through skilled craftsmanship. The result is furniture that feels both architectural and effortlessly refined.

Care & Maintenance

Our collections are designed to evolve beautifully over time. Teak will soften into a silvery patina, while powder-coated aluminium and woven elements retain their integrity with gentle care. With simple attention, each piece continues to embody understated elegance year after year.
