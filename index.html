The Amy Ann Story

Amy Ann Living was created with a simple belief: outdoor spaces deserve the same consideration, craftsmanship, and design integrity as the interiors we live in every day.

Inspired by modern villas, coastal retreats, and the effortless elegance of resort living, Amy Ann Living curates luxury outdoor furniture designed to elevate life outdoors, season after season.

Each piece is defined by timeless form, premium materials, and a commitment to enduring quality. From sustainably sourced teak to performance fabrics and architectural aluminium, every detail is selected to withstand sun, heat, rain and time without compromising on comfort or aesthetics.

Designed for private residences, poolside settings, and refined outdoor environments, our collections blur the line between indoor and outdoor living, created for moments of calm, connection, and escape.
